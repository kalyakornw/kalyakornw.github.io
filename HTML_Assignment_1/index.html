Inside Out

Director: Pete Docter

Genre: Animation

Year Released: 2015

Award: Best Animated Feature Film of the Year 2016
Fun Face: Riley is Based on the Director's Daughter

Inside Out is a 2015 Pixar film about the five core emotions
Joy, Sadness, Fear, Anger, and Disgust—that live inside
the mind of an 11-year-old girl named Riley.
When Riley's family moves to San Francisco,
a chaotic journey ensues inside her mind as
the emotions struggle to guide her through this major life change.
